About Me

I'm a visiting Data Science Africa (DSA) Research Fellow, hosted at Centre for Data Science & Artificial Intelligence (DSAIL) in Nyeri, Kenya. I completed a BSc. Biomedical Engineering at Kenyatta University, Nairobi, Kenya (Graduating July 2024).

During my undergraduate, I have been fortunate to work as a research assistant under the guidance of Professor Ciira Maina at DSAIL in Dedan Kimathi University of Technology , Nyeri, Kenya and with Dr. Andrew Katumba and Dr. Jan Mikelson at Marconi Research and Innovations Lab in Makerere University , Kampala, Uganda, both experiences resulting to peer-reviewed publications.

Additionally, worked as a data intern at Talent Bridge Africa with Stella Nzisa, senior market researcher, and , Veronica George, business consultant, supporting clients with data-driven decision making insights.

I have also been fortunate to win scholarships to participate in person in international data science summer schools in countries like Greece, Ghana, Norway, Paris, Rwanda, as well as present a paper in Uganda and South Africa.

As a result, I have gained interest in contributing to the development of machine learning solutions. Therefore, I'm building skills in mathematics, programming, and scientific communication, and interested in pursuing a postgraduate degree in Artificial Intelligence (AI) to contribute solutions to my community and beyond.
