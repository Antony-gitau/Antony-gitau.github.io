Data Intern

Talent Bridge Africa | June 2020 - December 2020

As the date intern, my work involved; Collecting data from secondary sources, managing healthcare research database, updating research on Kenya's healthcare industry, creating weekly research reports and presentations for our clients, and collaborating remotely.

By the end of my internship, I had generated over 25 weekly reports on healthcare trends that supported investment decisions of our clients. Had an upto date database, involved in successfully running three projects concurrently in (online) collaboration with my colleagues.

Biomedical Engineer Attaché

Kiambu Level 5 Hospital | May 2019 - August 2019

As an attaché, I worked under the hospital's biomedical engineers to assemble new machines, troubleshoot faulty machines, conducting planned protective maintenance(PPM) on hospital's critical machines, analyzed electrical problem and designing new electrical circuits for installation of machines.

At the end of my attachment, I had installed over 5 new machines, conducted over 50 hospital faulty machines checking rounds, participated in PPM of the hospitals magnetic resonance imaging(MRI) equipment.
