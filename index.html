About Me

I am PhD Fellow at the University of South-Eastern Norway studying AI for hematological image analysis in collaboraton with Vestfold Hospital Trust, under the supervision of Profs. Veralia Gabriela , Karl Thomas Hjelmervik and Ola Marius Lysaker.

Previously, I graduated with a distinction in dissertation in MSc Mathematical Sciences (Artificial Intelligence for Science) from the University of Cape Town and the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), South Africa sponsored by Google DeepMind Education Scholarship. For my dissertation, I worked on multimodal-RAG based pipeline for medical education assessments at Jamieson Lab , Texas, USA.

I also completed a research internship at Google Reseach Africa , where i applied machine learning and causality to food insecurity challenges in Africa under the supervision of Dr. William Ogallo.

Before that, I was a Data Science Africa (DSA) Research Fellow, where I worked on computer vision approaches for monitoring recovery of orthopedic patients. I also contractually wrote technical articles for Zilliz to assist AI developers in using Milvus, a popular open-source vector database.

I hold a BSc. Biomedical Engineering from Kenyatta University, Nairobi, Kenya. During my undergraduate, I worked with Professor Ciira Maina at the Centre for Data Science & Artificial Intelligence (DSAIL), where I developed an orthopedic wearable device for recovery monitoring. I also visited Marconi Lab in Makerere University, Kampala, Uganda, where I worked with Dr. Andrew Katumba on developing Swahili speech recognition model, and with Dr. Jan Mikelson on breast cancer literature review for a hackathon participation at the University of Texas Southwestern. Both experiences resulted in peer-reviewed publications. I also interned at Talent Bridge Africa supporting clients with data-driven decision making insights.

I won multiple scholarships to participate in person in international data science events in countries like France, Norway, Greece etc. I also enjoy actively participating in communities such as KamiLimu, IEEE, Toastmasters, where I have organized events, lead new initiatives and mentored others.
